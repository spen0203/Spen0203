About Me!

I grew up in a tiny community in central Ontario, with little interest in livestock I found myself online researching technology starting from a young age. By the time I was in highschool I had studied computer repair and built my own computer. It was clear to me by this point I wanted to work with technology, Lucky for me my high school offered Co-op and intruduction to programming. During theese years I learnt to create basic java applications and to diagnose and repair various software and hardware problems. When I graduated I had one goal, to find a Software Enginering program that offered Co-op. This lead me to a Three year program at Algonquin College. Here I began my indepth programming journey learning several languages and concepts. I did one of my two available four month coops to gain an understanding of the corporate enviroment, but opted out of my second to allow me to pursue a more flexible web development job that would work with my school schedule.

Now that i'm set to graduate I hope to find a steady career preferably in downtown Ottawa or surronding area, I hope to find a workplace that will promote learning and push me to excel in everything I do not just programming. I want a career that may feel intimidating at times but will allow me to learn the most, one growth is promoted.
